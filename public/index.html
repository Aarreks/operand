Rules
  • Race for 120 seconds.
  • Addition uses 2..100 + 2..100.
  • Subtraction, division, and multiplication use Zetamac-style default ranges.
  • At 20, 40, 60, 80, and 100 seconds, a weighted wheel may spin.

  • The player not landed on must solve a negative answer challenge to finalize the shot and earns a point.
  • The landed-on player gets a red four-digit addition problem that does not score.
